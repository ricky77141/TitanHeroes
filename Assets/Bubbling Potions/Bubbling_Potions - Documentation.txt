This Bubbling Potions pack provides a selection of bubbling magic potions and chemistry props.

Load the Bubbling_Potions.unity for an example scene, or drag your chosen drink from the Prefabs folder in the Project window into the Hierarchy window.

For accurate glass reflections, don't forget to also included the Reflection_Probe prefab in your scene at the potion's location.

Have fun!
